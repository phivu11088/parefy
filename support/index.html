Getting Started

How do I import a PDF?
Tap the + button on the home screen and choose a PDF from Files, iCloud Drive, or any connected storage provider. You can also use the Share sheet from other apps to send PDFs directly to Parefy.
Does Parefy require an internet connection?
No. Parefy works entirely offline. All PDF processing happens on your device. No files are uploaded anywhere.
Does Parefy work on iPad?
Yes. Parefy is a universal app that runs natively on both iPhone and iPad.

Compress PDFs

What is target-size compression?
Target-size mode lets you set the exact file size you need (for example, 5 MB for an email attachment). Parefy's compression engine automatically finds the best strategy to reach your target while keeping text readable. Tap Compress, select Target size, and drag the slider or type a custom value.
What do the compression levels mean?
  • Keep Quality — minimal reduction, preserves original clarity
  • Balanced — good size reduction with high readability
  • Smaller File — significant reduction, slight quality trade-off
  • Extreme — maximum compression for the smallest possible file
For most documents, Balanced is the best starting point.
Will compression make my text blurry?
Parefy classifies each page (text, scanned, photo, mixed) and applies the right strategy for each. Text-heavy pages are compressed with structure-level optimizations that keep text sharp. Only image-heavy pages use raster compression, and even then quality floors prevent over-compression.
Why is the compressed file larger than expected?
Some PDFs are already well-optimized and can't be reduced further without quality loss. If Parefy can't beat the original size at your chosen quality level, it will keep the original to avoid making things worse.

Merge & Split

How do I merge PDFs?
Tap Merge on the home screen, select the files you want to combine, then drag to reorder them. Choose Append to combine back-to-back, or Interleave to alternate pages (useful for combining front/back scans). Tap Merge PDFs to create the combined document.
How do I extract specific pages?
Tap Extract, select a PDF, then choose the pages you want. You can pick individual pages, odd/even pages, or type custom ranges like 1-3, 7, 12-15. Tap Extract to create a new PDF with only those pages.

Sign Documents

How do I sign a PDF?
Tap Sign, select a PDF, then draw your signature on the canvas. Your signature is saved for reuse. On the PDF, pinch to resize, drag to position, and use two fingers to rotate. Tap Export when done.
Can I save multiple signatures?
Yes. You can create and save multiple signatures and choose which one to use each time you sign a document.

Scan & Photos to PDF

How does document scanning work?
Tap the scan button on the home screen and point your camera at a document. Parefy uses Apple's VisionKit for automatic edge detection, perspective correction, and image enhancement. You can scan multiple pages in one session.
Can I convert photos to PDF?
Yes. Tap Photos to PDF, choose photos from your library, and Parefy converts them into a single PDF document. You can choose quality presets to balance file size and image clarity.

Privacy & Security

Are my files uploaded anywhere?
No. Parefy processes everything on your device. There are no servers, no cloud uploads, no analytics, and no ads. Your documents never leave your iPhone or iPad. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Can I password-protect a PDF?
Yes. Tap Password on the home screen, select a PDF, and set a password. You can also unlock password-protected PDFs for editing.

Troubleshooting

The app can't open my PDF
Some PDFs may be damaged or use non-standard encoding. Try opening the file in Apple's Files app first to confirm it's valid. If the file is password-protected, use the Password tool to unlock it before using other tools.
Compression is taking a long time
Large PDFs (1,000+ pages) with many images take more time to process. The progress bar shows real-time status. You can cancel at any time without losing your original file. For very large files, try the Keep Quality or Balanced preset first — these are faster than aggressive compression.
Where are my exported files saved?
When you tap Save to Files, you choose the destination in the system file picker (iCloud Drive, On My iPhone, or any connected storage provider). You can also tap Share to send directly to another app, AirDrop, or email.
