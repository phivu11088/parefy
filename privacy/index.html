Privacy Policy

Effective: April 7, 2026 · Version 1.0

Parefy processes your PDFs entirely on your device. No files are uploaded to any server. No account is required.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Parefy does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Your PDF files, images, or document content
  • Your name, email address, or account credentials
  • Your location, contacts, or calendar data
  • Browsing history or behavioral tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers or cross-app profiles

2. On-Device Processing

All PDF operations — compression, merging, splitting, signing, extracting, watermarking, page management, and password protection — happen locally on your iPhone or iPad. Your files never leave your device through Parefy.

3. Optional Anonymous Analytics

Parefy includes an optional, off-by-default analytics feature powered by Firebase Analytics. You can enable it in Settings under "Help Improve Parefy."

When enabled, we collect bucketed, anonymized metrics only:

What We Collect Example What We Never Collect
File size range "5–20 MB" Exact file size or file name
Page count range "11–50 pages" Exact page count or content
Tool used & outcome "compress · success" File paths or document text
Duration range "~5 seconds" Precise timestamps
Device class & iOS version "iPhone · iOS 18" Device ID or user identity

You can disable analytics at any time in Settings. When disabled, no data is sent.

Firebase Analytics retains data for 14 months per Google's standard retention policy. For details, see Firebase Privacy.

4. Data Stored on Your Device

Data Storage Purpose
Imported PDFs App sandbox Your document library
Saved signatures App sandbox (PNG) Reusable signing
Thumbnail cache Caches directory Fast previews (clearable in Settings)
Monthly operation count Keychain Free tier limit tracking
Purchase entitlements UserDefaults + StoreKit Pro feature access
Temporary processing files /tmp (auto-deleted after 24h) Compression working files

All data stays on your device. Parefy does not use iCloud sync or any cloud storage.

5. Permissions

Permission When Requested Why
Camera Document scanning Capture pages to create PDFs
Photo Library (add only) Image export Save exported PDF pages as images

Parefy does not request access to your location, contacts, microphone, or any other sensitive data.

6. In-App Purchases

Parefy offers an annual subscription and a one-time lifetime purchase, both managed entirely through Apple's StoreKit framework. We do not process payments directly and do not have access to your payment information. Apple's purchase and subscription terms apply.

7. Children's Privacy

Parefy does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since no account or personal data is required, the app can be used by anyone without privacy concern.

8. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated is Firebase Analytics (Google), and only when you explicitly opt in. No other third-party SDKs, ad networks, or tracking services are used.

9. Data Security

Your files are protected by iOS's app sandbox, which prevents other apps from accessing Parefy's data. Monetization state is stored in the iOS Keychain with afterFirstUnlock protection. Temporary files are automatically cleaned up within 24 hours.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@parefy.com.
